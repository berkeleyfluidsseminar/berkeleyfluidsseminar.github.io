Friday, November 17, 2017

9 Lewis Hall, 14:00-15:30

Professor L. Mahadevan (Harvard)

Collective dynamics of super-organisms



Abstract: The speaker will discuss a range of problems involving active biological systems motivated by the ecophysiology of social insects that collectively behave as super-organisms. These include the morphogenesis and physiology of termite mounds, and active mechanisms for the self-organized ventilation, temperature control, and mechanical adaptation in bee hives.
