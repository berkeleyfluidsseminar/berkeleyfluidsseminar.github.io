The Berkeley Fluids Seminar series meets weekly on Mondays to discuss about the broad spectrum of cutting-edge fluid dynamics research. It is a great opportunity for the Fluids community in Berkeley (students, postdocs and faculty members) and for visitors to gather and share research thoughts in an informal context. The seminars will be hold on Mondays in 3110 Etcheverry Hall with a few exceptions. The schedule for the Spring 2019 is available here.


If you are willing to participate, we would be glad to have you give a seminar and meet the Berkeley Fluids community. Please feel free to contact us (contact on the left panel).
